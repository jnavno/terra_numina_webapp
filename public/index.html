Terra Numina

Welcome to Terra Numina

Sacred places are a weaving together of physical and metaphysical elements. They are created through spiritual practices and rituals and remain alive through the ages. They are made by the engagement of human consciousness with geological and natural elements. When you actively participate in one of these structures of luminosity and sound, you both fortify your subtle bodies and the integrity of the sacred place. Yet for many this subtle interaction remains beyond their perception. Through the practice of inner vision and other meditation techniques on sacred sites, Terra Numina offers opportunities for participants to see how their subtle bodies interact with the spiritual frequencies and forces rooted in that site.
