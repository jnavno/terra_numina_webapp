Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Terra Numina's Privacy Policy. Your privacy is critically important to us. Terra Numina is committed to safeguarding the personal data of our visitors, members, and users, whether you're accessing our website, taking part in our transformational journeys, or engaging with our educational courses on sacred sites and ancient knowledge. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information, as well as your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you interact with us:

  • Personal Identification Information: This includes your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and any other contact information you voluntarily provide when you sign up for our courses, newsletters, or other services.
  • Payment Information: For purchasing courses or other services, we collect payment details through secure third-party payment processors. We do not store or handle your full payment details directly.
  • Course and Event Participation: When you enroll in our courses, workshops, or events, we collect data on your participation, preferences, and any feedback or reviews you provide.
  • Website Usage Data: We use analytics tools to collect information on how you interact with our website, such as pages visited, time spent on each page, and clicks.

How We Use Your Information

Terra Numina uses the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize our services, including courses, events, and resources related to sacred sites and ancient knowledge.
  • To process your transactions and communicate about your orders or course enrollments.
  • To send periodic emails, including updates on new programs, promotional materials, and other relevant information.
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior, improving the usability and content of our site.
  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure the safety and security of our users.

How We Protect Your Data

We take data protection seriously. Your personal data is stored securely, and we implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your information. However, no online service is entirely immune to security risks, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and safeguard your accounts.

Third-Party Services

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party services to help us operate our website and services, including payment processors, email service providers, and analytics platforms. These third parties are required to handle your data in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws. Terra Numina does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

International Data Transfers

Terra Numina is a U.S.-based organization, and as such, any information you provide may be transferred to and processed in the United States. If you are accessing our services from outside the U.S., please be aware that data protection laws in your country may differ from those in the U.S. However, we take steps to ensure that your personal data is protected in compliance with applicable international standards.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under local data protection laws, such as:

  • Accessing the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correcting inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Requesting the deletion of your personal data.
  • Objecting to or restricting certain data processing activities.
  • Withdrawing consent to data processing where consent was previously provided.
If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@terranumina.org.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website by remembering your preferences and tracking user behavior. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us provide a personalized experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may affect your ability to use some features of our site.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Terra Numina reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@terranumina.org.
