Healing

Healing Through Sacred Sites and Earth Energies

Terra Numina offers a range of healing services based on the ancient knowledge of sacred sites and the medicine of the Earth. Our healing practices draw from a variety of traditions, utilizing the transformative power of sacred places to foster deep physical, emotional, and spiritual healing.

Energy Healing

Our energy healing sessions are designed to connect participants with the Earth’s subtle energies. These sessions take place at sacred sites or remotely, using the land’s vibrational frequencies to help balance the body's energy fields. This practice promotes holistic healing, revitalization, and inner peace.

Whether in-person or virtual, our energy healing sessions are tailored to the individual's needs, focusing on restoring harmony and alignment through sacred site energy work.

Sacred Site Medicine

We incorporate ancient medicinal practices from various indigenous traditions, using sacred plants, minerals, and rituals tied to sacred places. Our sacred site medicine sessions involve ceremonies that align participants with the healing power of the Earth, facilitating deep inner transformation and physical well-being.

Participants will have the opportunity to experience Earth-based healing techniques, such as herbal medicine, crystal work, and ceremonial practices rooted in sacred site knowledge.

Sound Healing

Our sound healing sessions use the natural acoustics of sacred sites to amplify the healing effects of sound. Participants are immersed in the vibrations of ancient instruments, chants, and nature’s own soundscapes to promote deep relaxation and spiritual connection.

Sound healing has been used for centuries to heal and transform, and in the sacred settings we use, the effects are magnified, providing an experience of deep resonance and tranquility.
