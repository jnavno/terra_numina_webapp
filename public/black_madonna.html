Black Madonna

Diving into the Mystery

We are a group of spiritually oriented people who's training and life experience has opened us up, each in our own directions, to the dynamic interconnectedness between humanity and the physical and spiritual aspects of the Earth. We guide participants through an experiential approach to sacred geometry which is concerned with the geographic location of sacred places according to various mythological, symbolical, astrological, geomantic and ritual factors.

History

Our vision is to promote awareness and preservation of sacred sites and natural environments. We believe that by understanding and respecting these places, we can foster a deeper connection with our world and each other.

Sacred Madonna

We are committed to building a community of like-minded individuals who share our passion for sacred lands and transformational experiences. Join us in our events and discussions to be part of this meaningful journey.

Sites

We actively participate in efforts to protect and preserve the natural world. Our projects include conservation initiatives and educational programs aimed at promoting sustainability and environmental stewardship.
